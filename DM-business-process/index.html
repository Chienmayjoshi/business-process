Hey there!

Let's see how the development process works at DM.

These are the people who contribute to the process.

They all collaborate the work at one place called "Basecamp".

It's a Project management tool.

Biz devs communicate with the Clients

and finalise the requirements.

Biz devs ask Designers to design layouts as per the requirements.

Designers make awesome designs that suits the needs of clients.

After the approval, BD (Biz devs) makes a request to a Developer to develop it.

The smartly developed code goes for QA to test it for perfection with the help of Mantis.

A repetative process of bugs posting and resolving takes place.

Finally, HTML is mapped onto different domains.

This is how it works. Thank you for watching.
